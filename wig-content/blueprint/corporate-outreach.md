## Outreach Philosophy

These are cold emails. They should not read like cold emails.

No subject lines that try to be clever. No first paragraph that explains who you are for three sentences before getting to the point. Direct, professional, and immediately clear on value.

Each email is personalized by recipient type because the hook is different for each audience. The program is the same — the entry point is different.

Use these as written or adapt the opening two lines for a specific contact name or company.
