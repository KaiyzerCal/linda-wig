## BOOK 1: The ID Restoration Playbook ($9.99)

**Opening Hook:**
Nobody warned me that the first thing I'd need to rebuild my life was a piece of plastic with my face on it. They let me out with a gate check, a bus ticket, and the clothes I came in with. No state ID. No bank account. No way to prove I was who I said I was. I spent the first week of my freedom unable to do almost anything that required documentation — which turned out to be everything.

This book exists so that week doesn't happen to you.

**Chapter 5 — Your Social Security Number and Credit History**

## BOOK 5: The Mental Architecture of Freedom ($9.99)

You can walk out of a facility and still be incarcerated in your mind. The walls come down. The cells stay. I've watched people — smart, capable, determined people — get out and shrink back into patterns that didn't serve them inside and destroy them outside. Not because they wanted to. Because nobody taught them how to think differently than a confined system had taught them to think for years. This book is about the work that happens inside your head — which is where freedom is actually built or lost.
