## BOOK 1: The ID Restoration Playbook
**Price:** $9.99
**Tagline:** *Your identity was taken. Here's how to get it back — piece by piece.*

**Opening Hook:**
You walked out with nothing they could use. No ID, no Social Security card, no record of who you were before they had you. Before you can work, rent, bank, vote, or move — you need paper. This book is the paper map.

**Tone Notes:** Procedural but human. No bureaucratic language. Each step is written as if Bishop is sitting across from you at a table, walking you through it. Frustration is acknowledged — then redirected.

### Chapter Structure:

**Chapter 1 — Why This Comes First**
- The cascading effect of no ID: can't work, can't bank, can't rent
- Why most people skip this and pay for it later
- What documents you need and in what order
- How to move even when you're broke

**Chapter 2 — The Birth Certificate**
- How to order it by state (state-by-state resource included)
- What to do if you were born in a different state
- Fee waiver programs and reentry organization assistance
- Common delays and how to move around them

**Chapter 3 — Social Security Card**
- SSA office process step by step
- What to bring, what to say, what to expect
- Replacing a lost card vs. getting a new number
- What a Social Security card unlocks next

**Chapter 4 — State ID and Driver's License**
- DMV requirements by state overview
- License suspension: how to find out and what reinstatement costs
- Second chance ID programs in various states
- What to do when you can't afford the fees

**Chapter 5 — Passport (Yes, Even You)**
- Why a passport matters for employment and stability
- Conviction types that affect passport eligibility
- How to apply, cost, and timeline
- What a passport unlocks that other ID doesn't

**Chapter 6 — Voter Registration**
- State-by-state rules on conviction and voting rights
- How to re-register once eligible
- Why this matters beyond politics

**Chapter 7 — Record Expungement and Sealing**
- The difference between expungement, sealing, and pardons
- Which states allow which, for which offenses
- Free legal aid resources for expungement
- How to find out if you qualify

**Chapter 8 — Building Your Paper Trail**
- Creating a secure document file
- What to keep, what to copy, where to store it
- How to rebuild your credit identity

## BOOK 2: Housing While on Parole: What They Don't Tell You
**Price:** $9.99
**Tagline:** *The rules are real. The options are real. You need both.*

**Opening Hook:**
They'll tell you where you can't live. They won't always tell you where you can. This book fills that gap — because sleeping somewhere you're not supposed to is a violation waiting to happen, and sleeping in the street isn't a plan.

**Tone Notes:** Practical with full acknowledgment of how unjust the system is — but focused on what works within it. No idealism. The reader needs a roof, not a lecture.

**Chapter 3 — Staying With Family**

**Chapter 4 — Sex Offender Restrictions and Housing**

**Chapter 6 — Private Landlords and Background Checks**

- States with fair chance housing laws
- What a landlord can legally ask and what they can't
- Building a rental history when you have none

**Chapter 7 — Emergency and Short-Term Options**
- Shelter systems and how to navigate them
- Emergency housing through 211 and local nonprofits

**Chapter 8 — Building Toward Permanent Housing**

**Chapter 7 — The First 90 Days on the Job**
- How to show up when you're building from zero

**Chapter 8 — Long-Term Career Strategy**

**Price:** $9.99
**Tagline:** *Your body walked out. Now it's time to bring the rest of you.*

**Opening Hook:**
They can't lock up your mind. But incarceration gets in there anyway. The institutionalization is real, the hypervigilance is real, the grief is real, and the shame is real. This book is about building the mental structure you need to be free — not just released.
